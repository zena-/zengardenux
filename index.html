Zen Garden

The Challenge

To create a tool for freelancers in a service backed by DNA info

DNA info is the leading neighborhood news source-covering entertainment, eduction, politocs, crime, sports and dining.
Zen Garden is my first feature of this service

This is a moving and delivery service that takes away the added stress that surround moving. Instead, this app brings peace of mind.

This was the goal of the project. To create a tool for people with busy lives; to help them moves their stuff from point A to point B.
My target audience are people on the move. Those between the ages of 18 and 40, who are on their phones regularly.
People who are moving in and out of university and apartments. Or, who are in an industry that requires them to move a lot of equipment to and from different location.
